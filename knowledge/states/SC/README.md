# South Carolina Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: SC Law Enforcement Division (SLED)
- **Website**: https://www.sled.sc.gov

## License Types

| Type | Training | Fee | Period |
|------|----------|-----|--------|
| Unarmed | 6 hours | $65 | **1 year** |
| Armed | 10 hours | $110 | **1 year** |

## Unarmed (Primary Basic)
- 4 hours SLED training + 2 hours company orientation
- Pass written exam (85%)
- Observe and report authority only

## Armed
- Complete unarmed first
- 4 additional hours firearms training
- Demonstrate proficiency

> [!NOTE]
> Only handguns permitted. Rifles/shotguns not allowed for security guards.
