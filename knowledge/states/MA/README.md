# Massachusetts Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Massachusetts State Police Certification Unit
- **Website**: https://www.mass.gov/state-police

> [!NOTE]
> MA does NOT require individual licenses for unarmed guards. Security agencies are licensed; guards work under agency.

## Unarmed Guards
- No state-issued individual license
- Training at employer's discretion
- Background check + fingerprinting required

## Armed Guards
- 21+ years old
- License to Carry (LTC) from local police
- 20 hours additional firearms training
- Annual firearms re-qualification

## Agency License
- Fee: $550 (renewal $150)
- Surety bond: $5,000
