# Connecticut Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Dept of Emergency Services and Public Protection (DESPP)
- **Unit**: Special Licensing and Firearms Unit (SLFU)
- **Website**: https://portal.ct.gov/DESPP

## License Types

| Type | Training | Period | Min Age |
|------|----------|--------|---------|
| Unarmed (Guard Card) | 8 hours | **5 years** | 18 |
| Armed (Blue Card) | 38 hours | **5 years** | 21 |

## Unarmed Guard Card
- 8 hours state-approved training
- Pass written exam (70%)
- Fees: ~$188 total

## Armed Guard (Blue Card)
- Must hold unarmed card + CT Pistol Permit
- 30 hours weapons training + 8 hours qualification
- **8-hour annual review** with firearm qual
