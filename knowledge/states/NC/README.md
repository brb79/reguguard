# North Carolina Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Private Protective Services Board (PPSB)
- **Parent**: NC Department of Public Safety
- **Website**: https://www.ncdps.gov/our-organization/law-enforcement/private-protective-services-board

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Min Age |
|------|------------------|---------|
| Unarmed Guard | 16 hours | 18 |
| Armed Guard | 36 hours | 21 |

## Unarmed Training Timeline
- **4 hours** within 20 calendar days of placement
- **16 hours total** within 30 days of hire

## Armed Guard Requirements
- Complete 16-hour unarmed training first
- 20-hour PPSB Firearms Course (classroom + range)
- Pass written test + day/night firing (80% minimum)
- Certificate must be dated within 90 days of application
