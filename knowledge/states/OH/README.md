# Ohio Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Ohio Private Investigator Security Guard Services (PISGS)
- **Parent**: Ohio Homeland Security
- **Training**: Ohio Peace Officer Training Commission (OPOTC)
- **Website**: https://homelandsecurity.ohio.gov/pisgs/

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------------|---------|
| Registered Guard (Unarmed) | **None mandated** | None | 18 |
| FAB Endorsement (Armed) | 20 hours | 4 hours/year | 21 |

> [!NOTE]
> Ohio does NOT mandate training hours for unarmed guards. Employers set their own training standards.

## Unarmed Guards
- Employer registers with PISGS within 7 business days
- Background check via Ohio BCI
- PISGS ID card issued after clearance

## Armed Guards (FAB Endorsement)
- 20-hour OPOTC handgun training
- +5 hours for shotgun (if carried)
- Initial certificate valid 18 months
- **Annual 4-hour requalification** (valid 12 months)
