# Michigan Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs (LARA)
- **Website**: https://www.michigan.gov/lara

## Key Difference

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Michigan does **NOT** issue individual security guard licenses or "guard cards."
> Guards operate under their employer's agency license.

## Structure
- Security agencies are licensed by LARA
- Individual guards register through employer
- Employer conducts background check (ICHAT)
- **No state-mandated training hours** for unarmed guards

## Requirements
- 18+ years old
- Work authorization
- Clean criminal record
- Fingerprint background check
