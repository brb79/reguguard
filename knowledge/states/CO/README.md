# Colorado Security Guard Licensing

## Key Difference

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Colorado has **NO statewide security guard license**. Licensing is handled by individual cities/counties.

## Local Requirements (Examples)

| City | Training Hours |
|------|----------------|
| Denver | 16 hours |
| Colorado Springs | 8 hours |
| Other areas | Varies/None |

## General Requirements
- 18+ years old
- Background check
- Contact local City Clerk's Office

## Armed Guards
- Meet local unarmed requirements
- Valid Colorado CCW permit
- Peace officer-equivalent firearms training

## Pending Legislation
HB 25-1262 would create statewide licensing but was "Postpone Indefinitely" as of 2025.
