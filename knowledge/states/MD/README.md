# Maryland Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Maryland State Police Licensing Division security guard and Special Police Officer requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Maryland State Police Licensing Division (MSPSL)
- **Training Commission**: Maryland Police Training and Standards Commission (MPTSC)
- **Website**: https://mdsp.maryland.gov

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------------|----------------|---------|
| Security Guard Certification | 12 hours | 8 hours | 3 years | 18 |
| Special Police Officer | 80 hours | 12 hours | 3 years | 21 |

## Important Changes

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Effective January 1, 2025**: All original security guard applicants must complete 12 hours of initial training from an MPTSC-approved provider.

> [!NOTE]
> Current security guards who hold valid certification but have not completed the 12-hour initial training will be required to do so for renewal.

## Security Guard Certification

### Initial Requirements
- 12 hours of initial security guard training (MPTSC-approved)
- FBI fingerprint background check
- Submit certificate of completion with application

### Renewal Requirements
- 8 hours continuing security training (MPTSC-approved)
- Livescan/FBI fingerprints
- Submit renewal 90+ business days before expiration

## Special Police Officer

### Initial Requirements
- 80 hours approved training (effective 10/1/2020)
- Criminal law instruction
- Constitutional procedural requirements (search, seizure, arrests)
- Appropriate use of force training
- FBI fingerprint background check

### Renewal Requirements
- 12 hours in-service training (MPCTC-approved)
- FBI fingerprints
- $60 renewal fee
- Submit renewal 90+ business days before expiration
