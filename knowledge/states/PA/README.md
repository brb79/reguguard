# Pennsylvania Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Pennsylvania State Police (PSP) security guard and Act 235 Lethal Weapons Certification requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Pennsylvania State Police (PSP)
- **Unit**: Lethal Weapons Certification Unit
- **Website**: https://www.psp.pa.gov

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------------|----------------|---------|
| Security Guard (Unarmed) | 40 hours | None | None | 18 |
| Act 235 Certification (Armed) | 40 hours | 8 hours | **5 years** | 18 |

> [!NOTE]
> Pennsylvania does not mandate state registration for unarmed security guards. Requirements are typically employer-driven.

## Act 235 Lethal Weapons Certification

Required for any private security personnel carrying firearms, batons, or other lethal weapons.

### Pre-Training Requirements
1. Submit application via TACS (Training And Certification System)
2. FBI fingerprint background check
3. Physical examination by PA-licensed MD
   - Vision: 20/70 uncorrected â†’ correctable to 20/20
   - Normal color and depth perception
4. Psychological examination by PA-licensed psychologist
5. Receive approval letter from PSP

### Training Breakdown (40 hours)

| Component | Hours |
|-----------|-------|
| Classroom (crime codes, legal authority, use of force) | 22 hrs |
| Armed/unarmed defense | 4 hrs |
| Firearms training and qualification | 14 hrs |
| **Total** | **40 hrs** |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Training must be completed within 6 months of receiving PSP approval letter.

### Fees

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Application | $50 |
| FBI Background Check | $30.25 |
| Certification | $30 |

### Renewal
- 8-hour recertification course every 5 years
