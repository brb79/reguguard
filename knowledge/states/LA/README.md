# Louisiana Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: LA State Board of Private Security Examiners (LSBPSE)
- **Website**: https://lsbpse.com

## License Types

| Type | Training | Period | Min Age |
|------|----------|--------|---------|
| Unarmed | 16 hours | 2 years | 18 |
| Armed | 16+ hrs + firearms | 2 years | 21 |

## Unarmed Training (16 hours)
- **8 hours** within 30 days of employment
- **8 more hours** within 60 days
- Pass 50-question exam (70%) after each segment

## Armed
- Complete unarmed requirements
- Firearms training by state-licensed instructor
- Range qualification with specific weapon
- **Annual retraining** (80% marksmanship)

## Reciprocity
Out-of-state officers: 4-hour training + exam if comparable license
