# Washington DC Security Officer Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Washington DC Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs (DCRA) Security Officer Management Branch (SOMB).

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Security Officer Management Branch (SOMB)
- **Parent**: Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs (DCRA)
- **Website**: https://dcra.dc.gov

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------------|----------------|---------|
| Security Guard (Unarmed) | 40 hours | None | 2 years | 18 |
| Special Police Officer (Unarmed) | 56 hours | 8 hours/year | 2 years | 18 |
| Special Police Officer (Armed) | 96 hours | 8 hours/year | 2 years | 21 |

## Training Breakdown

### Security Guard (Unarmed)
- 24 hours pre-assignment training
- 16 hours on-the-job training (within 90 days)

### Special Police Officer (Unarmed)
- 16 hours arrest powers, search/seizure, use of force
- 24 hours general duties (terrorism, emergency, customer service)
- 16 hours on-the-job training (within 90 working days)

### Special Police Officer (Armed)
- All unarmed SPO training (56 hours)
- 40 hours Firearms Certification
- Range qualification

## Key Requirements

- FBI background check required
- Drug screening required
- Physical examination from physician
- Pass SOMB certification exam (Pearson VUE)
- U.S. citizen or legal resident with valid work permit
