# Washington State Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Washington State Department of Licensing (DOL)
- **Firearms**: Washington State Criminal Justice Training Commission (WSCJTC)
- **Website**: https://www.dol.wa.gov/business/security

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Annual Training | Period |
|------|------------------|-----------------|--------|
| Unarmed | 16 hours | 4 hours | 2 years |
| Armed | 24 hours | Requalification | 2 years |

## Unarmed Guard
- **8 hours** pre-assignment training
- **8 hours** post-assignment within 12 months
- **4 hours** annual refresher
- Must have employment offer from licensed company
- IdentoGO fingerprint background check

## Armed Guard
- Must hold unarmed license first
- 21+ years old
- **8-hour WSCJTC firearms certification**
- Qualify with each firearm type
- Annual requalification
- Additional fee: $10
