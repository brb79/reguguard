# Arizona Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: Arizona Department of Public Safety (DPS)
- **Website**: https://www.azdps.gov/services/public/securityguard

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal | Period |
|------|------------------|---------|--------|
| Unarmed | 8 hours | 8 hours | 2 years |
| Armed | 24 hours | Annual + biennial | 2 years |

## Unarmed Guard
- 8-hour pre-assignment training (online or in-person)
- FBI/DOJ background check + fingerprinting
- Apply through registered security agency
- Fees: $72 total

## Armed Guard
- Complete unarmed training first
- **16-hour armed course (in-person only)**
- Live-fire range qualification + written test
- Annual refresher required
- Fees: ~$122-135
