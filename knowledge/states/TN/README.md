# Tennessee Security Guard Licensing

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: TN Dept of Commerce & Insurance (TDCI)
- **Division**: Private Protective Services

## License Types

| Type | Training | Renewal | Period |
|------|----------|---------|--------|
| Unarmed | 4 hours | 2 hours | 2 years |
| Armed | 16 hours | 4 hours | 2 years |

## Unarmed (4 hours)
- Orientation, legal powers, emergency procedures, duties
- TBI/FBI fingerprint check

## Armed (16 hours total)
- 4 hours basic + 8 hours firearms + 4 hours marksmanship
- Must score 70% on silhouette target
- 21+ years old
