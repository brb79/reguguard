# Florida Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (FDACS) security guard licensing.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (FDACS)
- **Division**: Division of Licensing
- **Website**: https://www.fdacs.gov/Divisions-Offices/Licensing

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------------|----------------|---------|
| Class D (Unarmed) | 40 hours | None | 2 years | 18 |
| Class G (Armed) | 28 hours | 4 hours | **1 year** | 18/21 |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Class G (armed) requires **annual renewal** with 4-hour firearms requalification, unlike Class D which is biennial.

## Class D Security Officer (Unarmed)

### Initial Requirements
- 18 years of age or older
- 40 hours FDACS-approved training
- Pass written exam (75% on 170-question test)
- Criminal background check with fingerprinting
- U.S. citizen or legal resident

### Renewal ($45)
- Renewal notice sent 95 days before expiration
- No continuing education required

## Class G Statewide Firearm License (Armed)

### Initial Requirements
- Must hold or be obtaining Class D license
- 28 hours firearms training with Class K instructor
- Range qualification (144 rounds)
- Written exam
- Psychological and physical exam (temp G license)

### Annual Renewal ($112)
- 4 hours firearms requalification
- Training with licensed instructor

## Expiration Consequences

| Time Expired | Consequence |
|--------------|-------------|
| 0-3 months | Late fee applies |
| 3-12 months | New application + fingerprints |
| 12+ months | Must retake 40-hour training |
