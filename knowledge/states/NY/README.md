# New York Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for New York Department of State (DOS) security guard registration requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: New York Department of State (DOS)
- **Training Authority**: Division of Criminal Justice Services (DCJS)
- **Website**: https://dos.ny.gov/security-guard

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Annual Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|-----------------|----------------|---------|
| Security Guard Registration | 24 hours | 8 hours | 2 years | 18 |
| Armed Guard Registration | 71 hours | 16 hours | 2 years | 18 |

## Unarmed Security Guard Training

| Phase | Hours | Deadline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Pre-assignment training | 8 hrs | Before registration |
| On-the-job training | 16 hrs | Within 90 days of employment |
| **Total Initial** | **24 hrs** | |
| Annual in-service | 8 hrs | Each calendar year |

## Armed Guard Training

Prerequisite: Valid NYS Pistol Permit + Unarmed Guard Registration

| Component | Hours |
|-----------|-------|
| All unarmed training | 24 hrs |
| Firearms classroom (Penal Law Article 35) | 7 hrs |
| Firearms range instruction | 40 hrs |
| **Total Initial** | **71 hrs** |

### Annual Requirements (Armed)
- 8 hours firearms training
- 8 hours in-service training
- **Total: 16 hours/year**

## Exemptions for Retired Law Enforcement

| Separated | Exemptions |
|-----------|------------|
| < 10 years ago | Exempt from 8hr pre-assignment, 16hr OJT, 47hr firearms |
| 10+ years ago | Must complete 8hr annual in-service |

## Important Notes

- Training schools submit completion records directly to DCJS
- If registration expires 6+ months, must reapply as new applicant
- Renewal forms mailed 90 days before expiration
