# New Jersey Security Guard Licensing (SORA)

## Regulatory Authority
- **Agency**: New Jersey State Police (NJSP)
- **Program**: Security Officer Registration Act (SORA)
- **Website**: https://www.nj.gov/njsp/sora/

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal Training | Renewal Period |
|------|------------------|------------------|----------------|
| Unarmed SORA | 24 hours | 8 hours | 2 years |
| Armed SORA | 24+ hours | 8 hours | **1 year** |

## SORA Training (24 hours)
- **8 hours** pre-assignment training
- **16 hours** on-the-job training
- Must complete within 30-day period

### Topics Covered
- NJ Attorney General Use of Force Policy
- Terrorism awareness
- Cargo theft & critical infrastructure
- First aid awareness, ethics, report writing

## Renewal
- 8-hour refresher course
- Fee: $80.87
- Begin 30-90 days before expiration

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Armed officers require **annual** renewal vs biennial for unarmed.
