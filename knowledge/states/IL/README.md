# Illinois Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Illinois Department of Financial and Professional Regulation (IDFPR) PERC card and armed security requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Illinois Department of Financial and Professional Regulation (IDFPR)
- **Website**: https://idfpr.illinois.gov

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Annual Training | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|-----------------|----------------|---------|
| PERC Card (Unarmed) | 20 hours | 8 hours | 3 years | 18 |
| Firearm Control Card | 48 hours | 16 hours | 3 years | 21 |

## PERC Card (Permanent Employee Registration Card)

Required credential for all security professionals in Illinois.

### Initial Requirements
- 18+ years old
- High school diploma or GED
- 20 hours IDFPR-approved training
- Fingerprint background check
- 8 additional site-specific hours within 6 months (for contractor agencies)
- Application fee: $55

### Annual Training: 8 hours

## Firearm Control Card (Armed)

### Prerequisites
- Valid PERC card
- Valid Illinois FOID card
- 21+ years old

### Training Requirements

| Component | Hours |
|-----------|-------|
| Basic security (PERC) | 20 hrs |
| Firearms training | 28 hrs |
| **Total Initial** | **48 hrs** |

### Annual Training: 16 hours
- 8 hours general training
- 8 hours firearms training
- Range re-qualification

Application fee: $75

## Recent Changes (Public Act 102-0152)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Effective July 2021:
> - 8 hours annual training required for ALL security officers
> - Armed guards: 28 hours firearms training (increased)
> - Armed guards: 16 hours annual (8 general + 8 firearms)
