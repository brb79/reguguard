# Georgia Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for Georgia Board of Private Detective and Security Agencies (GBPDSA) requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Georgia Board of Private Detective and Security Agencies (GBPDSA)
- **Parent**: Georgia Secretary of State
- **Website**: https://sos.ga.gov/page/private-detective-and-security-agencies

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Renewal CE | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|------------|----------------|---------|
| Security Guard (Unarmed) | 24 hours | 8 hours | 2 years | 18 |
| Security Guard (Armed) | 39 hours | 8 hours | 2 years | 21 |

## Unarmed Security Guard

### Initial Requirements
- 18+ years old
- High school diploma or GED
- 24 hours board-approved training
- Background check via GAPS (fingerprinting)
- Training can be online (Board-approved)

## Armed Security Guard

### Additional Requirements
- 21+ years old
- 24 hours basic training + 15 hours firearms training
- Valid Georgia Weapons Carry License (WCL)
- Annual weapon qualification (maintain range scores)

> [!NOTE]
> Firearms training cannot be completed online.

## Continuing Education (8 hours)

Required breakdown for renewal:

| Topic | Hours |
|-------|-------|
| Homeland Security | 2 hrs |
| Firearms-related | 2 hrs |
| Industry-related | 4 hrs |
| **Total** | **8 hrs** |

> [!TIP]
> First-time renewals are **exempt** from continuing education requirement.
