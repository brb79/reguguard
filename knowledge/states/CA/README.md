# California Security Guard Licensing

This directory contains regulatory documentation for California Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS) Guard Card requirements.

## Regulatory Authority

- **Agency**: Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS)
- **Parent**: Department of Consumer Affairs
- **Website**: https://www.bsis.ca.gov
- **Portal**: https://www.breeze.ca.gov

## License Types

| Type | Initial Training | Annual CE | Renewal Period | Min Age |
|------|------------------|-----------|----------------|---------|
| Guard Card (Unarmed) | 40 hours | 8 hours | 2 years | 18 |
| Firearms Permit (Armed) | 16 hours | 4 hours | 2 years | 21 |

## Guard Card Training Schedule

California allows guards to start work before completing all training:

| Phase | Hours | Deadline |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Pre-licensing (Powers to Arrest, Use of Force) | 8 hrs | Before work |
| Skills Training Phase 1 | 16 hrs | Within 30 days |
| Skills Training Phase 2 | 16 hrs | Within 6 months |
| **Total Initial Training** | **40 hrs** | |

## Continuing Education

- **8 hours annually** required for Guard Card
- Must include minimum 2 hours on use of force topics
- **4 hours annually** for Firearms Permit (in addition to Guard Card CE)

## Renewal Process

| Timing | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| 60-90 days before expiration | $40 |
| Up to 60 days after expiration | $65 |
| 60+ days expired | Must reapply as new applicant |

## Firearms Permit (Armed)

- Must already hold valid Guard Card
- 14 hours classroom + range instruction
- 2 hours assessment/requalification
- Range qualification required
